About Us.

Delivering Digital Excellence Since 2008

Founded in 2008 by Jasmine Scott, Hot Beans Web has grown from a small start-up into a successful digital agency with 36 employees. We now manage six major contracts with leading supermarkets and clothing brands, delivering reliable and high-performing digital platforms. We provide tailored digital solutions that help businesses strengthen their online presence and achieve measurable growth. Our core services include: Web Design & Development – Responsive, user-focused websites. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) – Increasing visibility and driving traffic. Branding & Digital Identity – Creating strong, memorable brands. E-Commerce & Shopping Trolley Systems – Secure online stores with integrated shopping functionality. Ongoing Support & Digital Strategy – Maintenance, optimisation, and expert guidance. At Hot Beans Web, we combine creativity, technical expertise, and innovation to help businesses succeed in today’s fast-moving digital world.
